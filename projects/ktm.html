KTM Product Catalogue

Business context

KTM is a Austrian motorcycle brand selling different types of bikes. This dashboard is an interactive webapp showing the product range of their naked bike models. On the app customers can click through the different models and select their preferred color. With this a customer can easily compare different bike models and have a clear overview of the most important technical specifications of the bike. The design of the app made sure that it is also optimized for usage on a mobile phone or tablet.

Tools used: Microsoft PowerBI
