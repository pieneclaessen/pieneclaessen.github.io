Who am I?

I am a (freelance) Data Scientist bridging business and technology by developing end-to-end data products that support operational decision-making.

From data pipelines and machine learning models to dashboards, I translate business needs into practical solutions and turn technical outcomes into actionable insights. With a background as a military pilot trainee, I bring a structured mindset and the ability to perform under pressure.

A selection of my recent work:

  • Developed a predictive maintenance ML model for the NH90 helicopter.
  • Built large language models (LLMs) for knowledge automation using RAG.
  • Designed and deployed Power BI dashboards and data products across multiple departments.
  • Developed predictive analytics to support decision-making.
  • Trained senior management in the strategic use of data and analytics.

In short, technical solutions that deliver tangible value and are actually used in day-to-day operations.
